About Me

Hi, my name is Michalis Papadakis. I am currently a Master's Student studying Robotics at ETH Zurich. My research interests lie at the intersection of dynamics, control theory, and optimization, aiming to answer the following question: How can model-based approaches enable robots to move and perform dynamic tasks in their environment efficiently, safely, and robustly? Specifically, I am interested in optimal and nonlinear control techniques, focusing on informed formulations that capture essential dynamics and leverage system properties while remaining mathematically well-conditioned for tractability in real-world applications. If you are interested in discussing my work, or regarding profesionnal opportunities, feel free to reach out to me at michael.d.papadakis@gmail.com.
